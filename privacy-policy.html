Privacy Policy

Premiere Voice AI processes user-provided text and voice settings solely for the purpose of generating and modifying voiceover audio within Adobe Premiere Pro.

The plugin may store generated audio files and related metadata locally on the user's device, including text, voice selection, tone, speed, pitch, filenames, and generation settings.

Premiere Voice AI does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

The plugin does not use user accounts, behavioral tracking, analytics, advertising technologies, or profiling systems.

All generated content and associated files remain under the user's control and can be deleted at any time.

Information processed by the plugin is used exclusively to provide voice generation, audio modification, import, editing, and workflow-related functionality.

By using Premiere Voice AI, you acknowledge and agree to the processing of information necessary to provide these features.
